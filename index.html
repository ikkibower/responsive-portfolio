About Me

Elliott Chalmers is an American businessman, inventor, and industrial designer. He was the co-founder, chairman, and chief executive officer (CEO) of Flava LLC.; CEO and majority shareholder of UNLTD, Inc; a member of Chalmers Enterprises board of directors following its acquisition of FlavaPC; and founder, chairman, and CEO of Tim's Atari Midi World. Chalmers is widely recognized as a pioneer of the microcomputer revolution of the 1970s and 1980s.
